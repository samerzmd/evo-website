Frequently asked questions

How do I start charging with EVO?

Download the app, top up your wallet, find a charger on the map, plug in and scan the QR code on the charger. Your session starts in seconds.

Which connectors and charger types are supported?

EVO supports both AC and DC charging across common connectors including CCS, Type 2 and CHAdeMO. The app shows which chargers match your vehicle.

How do I pay?

Charging is paid from your in-app wallet. You can top up with popular local methods such as Zain Cash, Dinarak, CliQ, UWallet and Orange Money, and apply vouchers or promo codes.

What are EVO points?

EVO points are loyalty rewards you earn every time you charge. Collect them and redeem rewards from the in-app catalog.

Is EVO available in Arabic?

Yes. EVO is fully bilingual in Arabic and English with right-to-left support and bilingual notifications.

Can I use EVO in my car?

Yes. EVO works with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, showing live prices, nearby stations and your active session on your car's screen.
